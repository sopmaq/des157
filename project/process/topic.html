2. Balikbayan visualization

Media art, landscape, and heritage

"Balikbayan" refers to the custom of immigrants and overseas workers sending household items and food purchased abroad back to the Philippines. I'd like to explore different ways that immigrants and their descendants communicate with their "home-country/countries" by creating some form of visualization or media representation.

With this piece of media art, I'd like to encourage a conversation about the differences between first and second/third/etc. generation immigrants, what can they learn from each other, and an understanding of personal experiences of immigration.
