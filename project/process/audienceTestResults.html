Audience Test Results


I received a lot of good feedback from David and Spencer. David surprised me when he mentioned that using the microphone felt "silly," but in a way that made him feel like he was in a relaxed environment. It was interesting watching David and Spencer play with the microphone as five minutes went by, and they both approached the microphone playfully and curiously - which may or may not be representative of someone under stress or experiencing anxiety.

Some of the changes to the visualization and design that I plan on making to my final are:

  • Start the screen off with instructions

  • Change the typography to something "more soothing" and less "geometric and rigid"



Some of the technical changes I plan on making to my final are:

  • Make the canvas responsive and span the width of the entire page

  • Create an option to toggle the breathing exercise on and off

  • Incorporate randomization for the visualization
