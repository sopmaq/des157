Audience Tasks

  1. Hold the headphone microphone underneath your nose for the duration of the activity.

  2. Complete the breathing exercise. Note that it may not be fully functional. If so, complete the following exercise:

    Breathe in for 4 seconds
    Hold for 7 seconds
    Breathe out for 8 seconds

  3. Find an on-campus resource for dealing with anxiety.

Questions for Tester

  1. Can you describe the interface you just used in one sentence?

  2. How did using the headset microphone feel?

  3. Did you understand purpose of the headset microphone?

  4. How does the color scheme of the site make you feel?

  5. How did you feel after completing the breathing exercise?

  6. Is the text legible?
